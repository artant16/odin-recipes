Berry Oatmeal

Image by Ivana Tomášková from Pixabay

Description

A very berry oatmeal recipe to start off your day with a healthy breakfast that will power your through your day.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 to 2 bananas, sliced
  • 1/4 cup frozen blueberries
  • 1/4 cup frozen raspberries
  • 1/4 cup frozen strawberries
  • Fresh berries of your choice (for topping)
    • Granola of your choice (for topping)

Steps

  1. Add all the ingredients to a saucepan over medium low heat.
  2. Gently stir and mix in the ingredients while they come to gentle simmer.
  3. Continue to stir as the oatmeal simmers for 3 to 5 minutes, or until it reaches your desired consistency.
  4. Once ready, pour into a bowl and let cool for several minutes.
  5. Top with fresh berries and granola of your choice and enjoy!
