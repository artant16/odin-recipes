Veggie Omelette

Image by mhburton from Pixabay

Description

An omelette packed with fresh veggies to give you a burst of goodness.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 mushrooms, peeled and chopped
  • Half a zucchini, chopped
  • 6 Kalamata olives, pitted and chopped
  • Small tomato, diced
  • Handful of baby spinach leaves
  • 50g feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tbsp of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Add two tablespoons of olive oil to the frying pan over medium to low heat.
  2. Add the feta, baby spinach leaves, tomato, olives, zucchini, mushrooms and eggs to a large bowl and mix.
  3. Add salt and pepper to taste, and combine ingredients.
  4. Pour the omelette mix into the frying pan and ensure it's spread out evenly.
  5. Wait two to three minutes and turn the omelette, turning off the stove and letting the omelette take the heat for several minutes.
  6. Turn the omelette over onto a plate and enjoy!
